Meet Our Founder

A Lifelong Love of Travel & History

"I hope to bring great joy to every client — through the places we visit and the memories we create together."

Stacey Minor's passion for travel began in her teenage years, spending summers exploring historical locations across the United States. Her first international trip at age 14 sparked a lifelong love affair with the world's most meaningful destinations.

With 10 years of experience as a trip manager — facilitating journeys to Israel, Europe, Asia, and Africa — Stacey founded JOY Tours to share that passion with everyone she meets.
